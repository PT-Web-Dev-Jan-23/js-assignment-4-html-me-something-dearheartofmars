Hello!

I am a multimedia artist, working primarily in painting and Sculpture. As an artist I want to reflect the beauty I see in the world in my work, from the beauty of extrordinary landscapes, to simple shadows I watch move across the wall in the morning. Even in my sculptural work which is often more abstract, I find inspiration from many things, crystaline structures, city planning concepts, and the spaces between trees in the woods. The inpiration is not always obvious in the finsihed piece, but where explanation ends, imagination takes over and I want to encourage the people who view my art to interpret it in their own way.
